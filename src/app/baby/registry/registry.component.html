A Note About Theme

A few people have asked us whether or not we have a nursery theme - the answer is, not really. We ask that you keep in mind we are having a boy, but otherwise we trust your taste - so have fun!!

What is on Our Registry

WHAT OUR REGISTRY DOES HAVE:

Our registry contains many of the big ticket items and smaller items which have been recommended or we've read reviews on and chosen according to our lifestyle and parenting plans. If something is on our registry it probably means we've put thought into that particular item.

WHAT OUR REGISTRY DOES NOT HAVE:

  • Diapers: We have not put diapers on our registry but this is a must have item! We could not receive too many diapers so we didn't think it was appropriate to put this item on a list which would be marked as "fulfilled" at any point. What we do ask you to keep in mind is that our son will grow quickly - A variety of sizes is appreciated!
  • Wipes: Much like diapers we didn't think it was right to have wipes on a list where it would be marked as "fulfilled" but we'll certainly need them!
  • Clothing: We have added a few particular clothing items to our registry that we wanted - otherwise, we have no specific taste or style (besides gender appropriate). As with the diapers we would love a range of sizes. Hand-Me-Downs: We are thankful for any generosity - not everything has to be new! If you want to gift us some used clothing items that are still in good condition we will gladly accept.
