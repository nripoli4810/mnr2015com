Books Rather Than Cards

We've heard so many great shower ideas over the past few months and, while we can't use them all, we loved this one:

We want to build up a great kids library at home! We would love if our friends and family, in lieu of buying a card, gave a favorite kids book and wrote a little message inside the front cover! Books could be new or lovingly used.

In order to help eliminate duplicate books, we've created a spreadsheet where you can see what books we already have. Once you have a book to bring in mind, please use the "Add A Book" button to add it to the list!
